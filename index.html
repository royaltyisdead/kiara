Freddie vs. Bri

Freddie

satisfaction first to drive customer loyalty and create long-term partnerships. Freddie consistently
exceeds sales targets, contributing significantly to the company’s growth and reputation.

Pros:

  • Personable
  • Always prepared
  • Passionate

Cons:

  • Multi-tasking
  • Snus addiction
  • Time Management
  • Work-Life Balance

Bri

Bri is a crucial part of Hyve's Law team as she is exceptionally driven, and knowledgable due to her
qualifications. She ensures compliance, mitigates risks, and provides priceless advice that all customers
appreciate in regards to their contracts. Her attention to detail in contracts and policies protects the
company from legal issues, while her ability to anticipate challenges and stay updated on legal trends
strengthens Hyde Managed Hosting’s position. Bri's specialised skills make her indispensable to the team.

Pros:

  • Organisation
  • Problem-solving
  • Better looking sibling
  • Good inntiative

Cons:

  • Can be easily stressed
  • Slower Decision Making
  • Salt addiction
  • Perfectionist

Freddie and Bri bring valuable and unique strengths to Hyve Managed Hosting. Freddie drives sales through his technical knowledge and relationship-building skills, though he occasionally struggles with time management and multi-tasking with all of his clients. Bri provides crucial legal protection and strategic insight, but her perfectionist tendancies and communication style may sometimes create longer work time of tasks. Both are integral to the Hyve's success, with Freddie excelling in client engagement and sales performance, and Bri ensuring legal customer compliance and legal alignment.
